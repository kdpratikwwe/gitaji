About This Sacred Collection

The Bhagavad Gita, composed of 700 verses, forms a precious manuscript within the great epic Mahabharata. This sacred text preserves a profound dialogue between Lord Krishna and the warrior prince Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

Our digital library presents these timeless teachings with reverence, offering each verse in its original Sanskrit form alongside transliterations and translations. Explore topics of dharma (duty), karma (action), bhakti (devotion), and the ultimate nature of reality.
